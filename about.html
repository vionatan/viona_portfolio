More about me!

I am a young UI/UX designer on a constant journey of learning and growth. As a lifelong learner, I love exploring the many ideas of user-centric design and leveraging the power of interesting UI design tools to create intuitive and delightful experiences.

Throughout my educational endeavors, I have cultivated a strong foundation in design principles, user research, and prototyping techniques. In my portfolio, you will find a collection of personal works and academic projects in which I would showcase my design process, projects ranging from website design to banners and icon designs. My general design process

After being exposed to many different design frameworks, I have adopted a design process that helps me facilitate my work. I would start off with a user research phase where I could find user challenges and empathise with the users to understand their needs in which afterwards I am able to define a problem statement. From then on, I would work on brainstorming many ideas with respect to the problem statement. With a choosen idea, I can work on the wireframing phase. generating drafts of wireframes and choosing the best elements to include into the final version. After validating the designs, I with turn them into a mock up and create a prototype out of it. Finally I would use the prototype to conduct a user testing to gather feedbacks, check the usability rate and for further improvements to the prototype.
