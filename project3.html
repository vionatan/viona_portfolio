Mobile App Design: TECH-KI

Overview

TECH-KI was a team project which is an app dedicated for students who need to buy laptops and other devices at a great deal as well as laptop servicing-related information. It also allows students to save their laptop warranty on the app so they do not have to worry about losing their hardcopy warranty.
